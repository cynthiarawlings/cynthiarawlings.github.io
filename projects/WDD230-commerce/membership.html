Membership Options

Non-profit: $100

  • Identified as a member of the Davenport Chamber member with window decal
  • A basic listing in the Davenport Chamber website business directory
  • A basic listing in the Davenport Chamber Relocation Guide & Directory
  • Use of Davenport Chamber logo in marketing and advertising materials
  • Community visibility & credibility as a Chamber member
  • Business development opportunities
  • Access to Chamber communications, including the weekly e-newsletter
  • Member 2 Member discounts
  • Legislative Advocacy
  • Access to advertising & promotional opportunities
  • Invitation to annual Signature Events
  • 1 Ribbon Cutting Ceremony with free social media posting
  • Access to US Chamber services & discounts
  • Office Depot National Chamber Program discounts
  • Referrals from the Chamber
  • Invitation to New Member Orientation Luncheon
  • Volunteer opportunities with committee participation

Bronze: $200

  • All Non-profit Benifits
  • 1 Anniversary Ribbon Cutting with free social media posting

  • 1 We Noticed Ceremony with free social media posting
  • New member recognition on social media
  • 6 Tickets to the Monthly Chamber Breakfast
  • Additional website directory listing
  • Complimentary certificate of origin & notary services

Silver: $400

  • All Non-profit and Bronze Benifits
  • 12 Tickets to the Monthly Chamber Breakfast
  • 1 week of Business Beat advertising
  • Complimentary member spotlight in the e-newsletter, once per year
  • Enhanced listing in Davenport Chamber website business directory
  • YouTube video posted to Chamber channel, video provided by the member
  • 2 Tickets to the Annual Taste of Davenport signature event

Gold: $1,000

  • All Non-profit, Bronze, and Silver Benifits
  • 4 Complimentary member mailing labels with Excel spreadsheet
  • Complimentary 4 weeks of Business Beat Advertising
  • An enhanced listing with business logo in Davenport Chamber website directory
  • Complimentary 2 weeks of Davenport Chamber website advertising
  • 24 Tickets to the Monthly Chamber Breakfast
  • 1 Promotion of an upcoming event on Chamber social media
  • Complimentary tickets to each of the following Signature Events: Taste of Davenport, Davenport Business Awards
  • Invitation to Annual Trustee Luncheon
  • 2 Promotions of upcoming events on Chamber social media
  • Business name on Trustee banner at all Chamber events
  • 25% discount on website & newsletter advertising
  • Recognition at Chamber Events, to include:
    • Business logo on Trustee banner displayed at all Chamber events
    • Logo on event programs & invitations
    • Recognition from the podium at each Monthly Chamber Breakfast
    • Acknowledgment from emcee at each Signature Event
  • Complimentary display table set up at one Chamber Breakfast per year
  • A featured story about your business, listed on the Chamber website and promoted twice on social media
  • Logo on the home page of Davenport Chamber website
