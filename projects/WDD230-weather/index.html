Wandering Weather

Wandering Weather is a personal project with the goal of bringing you the most up-to-date weather for Soda Springs, Fish Haven, and Preston; Idaho. All the images and information are free. The images are from Pexels. Our weather information is provided by Open Weather Map. The icons are from Iconfinder, they were created by Paolo Valzania.
